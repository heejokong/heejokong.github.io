Heejo Kong

Ph.D. candidate, Department of Brain & Cognitive Engineering, Korea University.

I am a graduated student pursuing a Ph.D. degree in Brain & Cognitive Engineering at Korea University, South Korea, and a member of Patter Recognition & Machine Learning Lab advised by Prof. Seong-Whan, Lee.

I am interested in exploring ways to enhance the practicality and reliability of machine learning systems for deployment in real-world applications.
